Use a machine learning model for movie recommendations!


* To leave this page and start the app, locate and click the words "Movie Ion" above.

* To access all features, log in using one of six preset usernames (user1, user2, user3, etc...)

* To choose the kind of recommendation (either best or worst matches), toggle the tan button above the movie posters. A black background (i.e. the default background) indicates search for best matches, a tan background indicates a search for worst matches.

* Select dropdown form located below movie posters.

* Type to search for desired movie.

* Once movie title appears in dropdown menu, click on desired movie title.

* Repeat the last three steps until three movies are selected.

* To remove a movie selection, type the movie title in search form and click title again to remove.

* Once three movies are selected, a green submit button will appear above dropdown menu.

* Find and click the green "submit" button to process your selections.

* Hover mouse over recommended movies to view their distance (hold your finger over movie if using touchscreen).

* Click on desired movie to view movie information.

* Click "add movie to watchlist" to track movie on your user profile (will need to be logged in).

* Click "Profile" to view watchlist. Click on the "x" located to the right of each movie to remove from list.
