Use a machine learning model for movie recommendations!


  1. To leave this page and start the app, locate and click the words "Movie Ion" at the top of the page.
  2. To access all features, log in using one of six preset usernames (user1, user2, user3, etc...)
  3. To choose the kind of recommendation (either best or worst matches), toggle the tan button above the movie posters. A black background (i.e. the default background) indicates search for best matches, a tan background indicates a search for worst matches.
  4. Select dropdown form located below movie posters.
  5. Type to search for desired movie.
  6. Once movie title appears in dropdown menu, click on desired movie title.
  7. Repeat the last three steps until three movies are selected.
  8. To remove a movie selection, type the movie title in search form and click title again to remove.
  9. Once three movies are selected, a green submit button will appear above dropdown menu.
  10. Find and click the green "submit" button to process your selections.
  11. Hover mouse over recommended movies to view their distance (hold your finger over movie if using touchscreen).
  12. Click on desired movie to view movie information.
  13. Click "add movie to watchlist" to track movie on your user profile (will need to be logged in).
  14. Click "Profile" to view watchlist. Click on the "x" located to the right of each movie to remove from list.
